# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Project participants are expected to communicate professionally, disagree on technical grounds without personal attacks, respect privacy, and avoid harassment or discriminatory behavior.

Unacceptable conduct includes intimidation, sustained disruption, sexualized language or imagery, publication of private information, and behavior that would reasonably make another participant feel unsafe.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers through GitHub or to
`prannay@warping.co`.
Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, restrict participation, or ban contributors
when needed to protect the project community. Reports will be handled as confidentially
as practical.

This policy applies in repository spaces and when representing the project in public forums.
